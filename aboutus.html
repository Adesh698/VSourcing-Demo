About us

          Unfortunately, Indian companies and organizations across all industries routinely compromise the accuracy, completeness, and uniformity of data when recording physical assets in a database. Furthermore, a high priority is not placed on tracking assets after acquisition. Asset record accuracy is further eroded when asset moves, changes, and disposals are not properly reflected. It limits the ability of financial and operational managers to leverage the information for business objectives and also exposes the business to risk in financial / tax / regulatory compliance.

          V Sourcing Asset Management services provide the foundation for improving asset management. Asset managers within organizations should be aware that optimally managing physical assets is extremely difficult without a comprehensive awareness of both the existence and utilization of physical assets. Organizations cannot afford to ignore the business benefits of V Sourcing services.

          The products we develop and services we provide are flexible enough to support the needs of companies that work in the fields of Education, Charity, Government, Manufacturing, Logistics, Finance, Retail, Medical and many more.

          We pride ourselves on the software and services we supply ensuring a long relationship where we ensure solutions fit the needs of your business.

          We are proud of the service provided by our Client Services team, whose knowledge is available to you by email vsourcingteam@vsourcing.co.in or www.vsourcing.co.in . They are available to answer queries relating to all of our products and services, and provide expert advice and guidance on all aspects of our product from start to end - asset management and much more.
